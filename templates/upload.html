✖

Polarity

Polarity in TextBlob quantifies the sentiment expressed in the text. It ranges from -1 to 1, where -1 indicates a very negative sentiment, 0 indicates a neutral sentiment, and 1 indicates a very positive sentiment. This allows you to gauge the overall emotional tone of the text, helping to understand whether it conveys positive, negative, or neutral feelings.

✖

Subjectivity

Subjectivity in TextBlob refers to the degree to which the text is opinionated. It ranges from 0 to 1, where 0 indicates a very objective statement (fact-based) and 1 indicates a very subjective statement (opinion-based). This helps to understand whether the content is expressing opinions or presenting factual information.
