Polarity and Subjectivity

Polarity: Polarity is a measure of how positive or negative a given piece of text is. The values range from -1.0 to 1.0, where -1.0 indicates a very negative sentiment, 0.0 indicates a neutral sentiment, and 1.0 indicates a very positive sentiment.

Subjectivity: Subjectivity indicates whether a text is more opinion-based or factual. The values range from 0.0 to 1.0, where 0.0 indicates a very objective text (factual), and 1.0 indicates a very subjective text (opinion-based).
